Privacy Policy

App: Dungeon Pixel Survivor
Effective date: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the mobile game Dungeon Pixel Survivor ("the App"), developed by Aiden ("we", "us"). The App does not require account creation or login, and we do not collect or store information that directly identifies you (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

1. Information Collected

We do not collect personal data directly. However, the following third-party services (advertising and payments) may automatically process certain data to provide their functions:

  • Advertising identifiers and device information — To serve ads, Google AdMob may process your advertising ID, device type and OS version, approximate location (based on IP address), and ad-interaction data.
  • Purchase information — When you buy the in-app product ("Remove Ads"), the Google Play billing system processes the transaction. We do not collect or store payment method details such as card numbers.

2. Game Progress (Save Data)

Game save data such as gold, character unlocks, and progression is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to or collected by us. Deleting the App also deletes this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Please refer to their respective policies for details on how they process data:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing (payments) — https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads). Purchasing the "Remove Ads" product stops in-app ads.

  • EEA / UK (GDPR): Where applicable, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data, and to object to or restrict its processing. Ad personalization in these regions is subject to your consent, which is requested within the App.
  • California (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. You may exercise your rights to know and to delete by contacting us below.

We may update this policy in response to legal or service changes. Updates will be posted on this page.
